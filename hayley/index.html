Quick Self-Care Guide

Save these steps to your phone or print them out! (Message me if you want a visual guide or have questions.)
  • Thumb Mobility Circles: Make a loose fist, move your thumb in a circle. 10 times each direction, 2-3x/day.
  • Tendon Glides: Open your hand, make a fist, then open again. Repeat 10 times, 2x/day.
  • Palm Massage: Massage the thick muscle at the thumb base for 2-3 minutes, 1-2x/day.
  • Warm Water Therapy: Soak your hand in warm water for 10 min (with Epsom salt if you have it), once daily.
  • Grip Strength: Squeeze and release a soft stress ball or putty, 10 reps, 1-2x/day.
  • Bonus: Wrist/finger stretches (flexor, prayer, extensions, circles) ‚Äì 1 round daily.
  • Stop any movement if you feel sharp pain. Gentle discomfort is normal; sharp pain is not.
