Programme Overview

Welcome to Your Recovery Journey!

12 weeks dedicated to restoring your glute function, eliminating pain, and rebuilding your strength.

Phase 1: Activation & Stability

Weeks 1-4

Focus on re-establishing fundamental glute activation and mastering core movement patterns to build a solid foundation.

Focus: Foundation & Control

Phase 2: Strength Without Strain

Weeks 5-8

Progressively increase resistance and introduce more complex movements to build strength safely and effectively.

Focus: Building Capacity

Phase 3: Power & Integration

Weeks 9-12

Develop power and integrate your newfound strength into functional, everyday movements for lasting results.

Focus: Functional Performance
