As part of your onboarding, I’ll ask you to keep a full 7-day food diary — recording everything you consume. This includes the times, amounts, reasons for eating, and how you felt afterwards. This isn’t for me to judge you. In fact, it’s often the act of writing it all down — being fully honest — that already begins the transformation.

I’m not interested in perfection. I’m interested in your real habits. Because only when I understand what’s happening behind the meals, can I help guide you forward with clarity. This gives me powerful insight into your routines, triggers and potential pitfalls — all things we can address before they become problems. So do this for yourself — with honesty and without shame.
