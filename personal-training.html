How soon will I see results?

Expect energy and posture changes in 2–3 weeks, physical changes by 4–6. Real transformation builds across 8–12+ weeks depending on effort.

Absolutely. I specialise in working with beginners, rebuilders and returners. We start where you are, and move forward — no intimidation, no pressure.
