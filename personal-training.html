About Personal Training

My personal training service is built on the principle that every body is unique, and so should be every fitness journey. I don't believe in one-size-fits-all programs or generic workouts. Instead, I create bespoke training experiences tailored specifically to your body type, fitness level, goals, and lifestyle.

Whether you're looking to build strength, lose weight, improve athletic performance, or simply feel better in your body, my personalized approach ensures you get exactly what you need to succeed. Each session combines cutting-edge exercise science with motivational coaching to push your limits and unlock your full potential.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I train to see results?

For optimal results, I recommend 2-3 sessions per week, but this varies based on your goals, current fitness level, and lifestyle. During your initial consultation, we'll determine the ideal frequency for your specific situation. Even one session per week can yield significant improvements when combined with independent workouts following my guidance.

I'm a complete beginner. Is personal training right for me?

Absolutely! In fact, beginners often benefit the most from personal training as it establishes proper form and technique from the start. I specialize in creating comfortable, progressive programs for beginners that build confidence and capabilities without overwhelming you. We'll start exactly where you are and progress at a pace that challenges but doesn't intimidate you.

Where do the training sessions take place?

I offer flexible training locations to suit your preferences. Sessions can take place at my private studio in Surrey, at your home (travel fees may apply), or at a mutually agreed public gym. I also offer outdoor training sessions in local parks during favorable weather. The location will be discussed during your consultation to find what works best for you.

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients notice initial changes within 2-3 weeks, such as increased energy, improved mood, and better sleep. Physical changes typically become noticeable around 4-6 weeks, with significant transformations occurring within 8-12 weeks of consistent training. Your results timeline depends on factors including training frequency, nutritional adherence, recovery quality, and genetic factors.

Do you offer any guarantees?

I offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee for your first session. If you don't feel it was valuable or a good fit, you won't be charged. For long-term results, I can't provide specific guarantees as outcomes depend heavily on your commitment and adherence to the program. However, clients who fully follow my guidance consistently achieve impressive results, which is why I maintain a 98% success rate.
