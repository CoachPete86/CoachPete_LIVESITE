Food Diary Analysis

You’ll be asked to keep a full 7-day food diary — recording everything you consume: times, amounts, reasons and how you felt after each meal or snack. This must be 100% honest — there’s no judgement. In fact, it’s often the act of writing down your intake that already starts to shift behaviour.

This diary gives me insight into your routines, triggers and relationship with food — so we can build strategies that prevent pitfalls before they happen. In the premium package, you can also upload images of your meals, and I’ll personally break down your macro intake and send you the full analysis.

How fast will I see results?

Clients typically see mindset and routine improvements in 2–3 weeks. Physical and visible results begin around week 4. Big transformations occur over 3–12 months.
