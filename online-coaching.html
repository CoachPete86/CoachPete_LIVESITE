Frequently Asked Questions

How does online coaching actually work?

After signing up, you'll complete a comprehensive assessment covering your fitness history, goals, schedule, available equipment, and other relevant factors. I'll then create your personalized training program, which you'll access through a professional coaching app on your smartphone or computer. The app delivers your workouts, nutrition guidance, and allows you to log your progress and communicate with me. You'll follow the program independently, submitting regular progress updates and form videos for review. We'll have scheduled check-in calls to discuss your progress, make adjustments, and address any questions or challenges. The program evolves continuously based on your progress and feedback.

I don't have much equipment. Can online coaching still work for me?

Absolutely! One of the strengths of my online coaching is adaptability to different equipment situations. Whether you have a fully-equipped home gym, basic equipment like dumbbells and bands, or nothing at all, I can design an effective program for your circumstances. During our assessment, I'll learn exactly what you have access to and create workouts accordingly. If your equipment situation changes (like when traveling), I can quickly modify your program. I've successfully coached clients who train in commercial gyms, home setups, hotel fitness centers, and even with no equipment at all using bodyweight training approaches.

How does online coaching compare to in-person training?

Both approaches have their advantages. Online coaching offers greater flexibility, allowing you to train on your own schedule rather than booking specific time slots. It's also more cost-effective while still providing personalized guidance. The biggest difference is that you'll execute workouts independently, with form corrections happening through video review rather than in real-time. This actually helps many clients develop greater body awareness and exercise autonomy. Online coaching also provides more consistent support throughout the week rather than just during sessions. Many clients find they achieve comparable or even better results with online coaching due to the flexibility promoting greater consistency.

How quickly will I see results with online coaching?

Most clients notice initial changes within 2-4 weeks, including increased energy, improved sleep quality, and enhanced mood. Visual changes typically become noticeable around 6-8 weeks, with significant transformations occurring within 3-6 months of consistent effort. Your results timeline depends on several factors including your starting point, goals, adherence to the program, nutrition compliance, and genetic factors. The key advantage of online coaching is the consistency it enables—you can train regardless of travel, work schedule changes, or other life events that might normally disrupt your fitness routine. This consistency is what drives results more than anything else.

What if I need to take a break or pause my coaching?

Life happens, and I understand that. For monthly subscriptions, you can pause your coaching for up to 30 days once per year, effectively extending your service period. For longer-term packages, you can pause for up to 60 days during your program duration. During pauses, you'll maintain access to your existing program and materials but won't receive new programming or check-ins until you resume. If you need to take an extended break beyond these allowances, we can discuss options including partial credits toward future services. My goal is to be flexible while ensuring you maintain momentum toward your goals.
