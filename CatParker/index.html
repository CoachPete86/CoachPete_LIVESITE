The Problem

When glutes stop firing, the body begins to compensate. Your quads, lower back, hip flexors, and feet take over roles they’re not designed for. The result? Pain – in knees, hips, and feet. It’s not random. It’s a chain reaction caused by glute dysfunction.

This programme maps and fixes that compensation cascade step-by-step, restoring balance and stopping the overload loop.

The Science

The glutes aren’t just for strength – they’re stabilisers, postural guardians, and neuromuscular drivers. When they switch off (from sitting, injury, pain), your system enters dysfunction.

This programme re-educates both the muscle and the nervous system through controlled, intelligent activation and progression. We use evidence-backed movement systems and research-proven protocols from physiotherapy and sports science.
