What is Group Training?

Group training combines expert coaching with community motivation. The atmosphere created when you train alongside others can drive effort, encourage consistency, and transform your workouts from a task into a high-energy, fulfilling experience. My sessions are engineered to challenge and support you — whatever your current level.

Whether you're joining solo or with friends, you'll never feel lost or overlooked. My groups are deliberately small so that everyone receives proper technique guidance, individualised encouragement, and smart progression.

FAQs

Can beginners join?

Yes — everyone starts somewhere. I scale every exercise to your level. Whether you're new or returning, you’ll be safe and supported.

Is this like a fitness class?

No — this is coaching. Not a workout you're thrown into. I keep numbers small, tailor movements, and build rapport with each member. You're coached, not just cued.

Can I bring friends?

Absolutely. Build your own squad and let’s create a time slot for your group. Or join an existing group — it’s all about finding the right fit.

What if I miss a session?

You’ll have access to the workout via my app, or you can reschedule if space allows. The goal is consistency, but I’ll work with your real life schedule.
