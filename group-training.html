About Group Training

My group training sessions combine the expertise of professional coaching with the motivational power of community. Working out alongside others creates a unique energy that drives performance, accountability, and enjoyment—often pushing you further than you'd go alone. These dynamic sessions are designed to deliver maximum results in a social, supportive environment.

Each session is carefully structured to accommodate varying fitness levels while ensuring everyone is appropriately challenged. You'll benefit from personalized attention within a group setting, learning proper technique, pushing your limits, and building connections with like-minded individuals who share your commitment to health and fitness.

Frequently Asked Questions

I'm a beginner. Will I be able to keep up with the group?

Absolutely! My group sessions are specifically designed to accommodate various fitness levels simultaneously. Every exercise has multiple progression and regression options, allowing beginners and advanced participants to train effectively side by side. I'll help you select the appropriate intensity level for each movement, and you'll never be pushed beyond what's safe or productive for your current fitness stage. Many beginners actually find they progress faster in a group environment due to the motivational aspect.

What's the difference between your group training and typical fitness classes?

Unlike large fitness classes where individual attention is minimal, my group training maintains a maximum 8:1 client-to-coach ratio, ensuring personalized guidance. Standard classes often use one-size-fits-all approaches, while my sessions provide individual movement modifications, intensity adjustments, and progress tracking. Additionally, my groups develop strong supportive communities with consistent membership, rather than constantly changing participants. You get the benefits of personalized coaching combined with the motivation and affordability of group formats.

Can I create a private group with my friends/colleagues?

Yes! Private group sessions are a popular option. You can form your own training group with 2-8 people of your choosing—friends, family members, colleagues, or neighbors. I can schedule these sessions at times that work for your group, including early mornings, evenings, or weekends. Private groups often develop special camaraderie and can be customized to address specific goals or interests shared by your group. Contact me to discuss creating your own training crew.

Where do group sessions take place?

Group training sessions are held at various locations depending on the season and format. Indoor sessions take place at my fully-equipped training studio in Surrey. During favorable weather, we often utilize local parks and outdoor spaces for refreshing open-air workouts. For corporate or private groups, I can also bring the training to your location if suitable space is available. All locations are carefully selected to ensure a safe, effective training environment with appropriate equipment and facilities.

What if I have to miss a session?

Life happens, and I understand that. If you're on a monthly package and need to miss a session, you can reschedule to another group session within the same month, subject to availability. For unplanned absences, I provide the workout details via the mobile app so you can complete it independently if you wish. I recommend aiming for consistent attendance as the group dynamic is a powerful motivator, but my flexible rescheduling policy ensures you get full value from your investment.
