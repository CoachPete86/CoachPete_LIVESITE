Why Group Training?

There’s something powerful about shared effort. Group training delivers the coaching quality of personal sessions with the energy, motivation, and community of a team. You’ll train smarter, sweat harder, and leave every session buzzing.

What Makes These Different

  • Coach-led from start to finish — no guessing, no winging it.
  • Planned progressive structure — each week builds on the last.
  • Training blocks that mix strength, conditioning, and mobility.
  • Focus on proper technique and form — not just keeping up.
  • Personal modifications to suit all levels, injuries, and needs.

Session Types

SWEAT 45: High-intensity, no-nonsense fat-burning circuits using a mix of dumbbells, kettlebells, sleds, rowers, and bodyweight drills.

ENGINE: Cardio-focused workouts to improve heart health, VO2 max, and endurance with ski ergs, rowers, and bikes.

TEAM TRAIN: Partner or small-group drills designed to challenge teamwork and motivation. You’ll go further with others in your corner.
